StreamSaavy Web

Lightweight browser interface for yt-dlp downloads. Works anywhere Python runs – including Android/Termux – by exposing StreamSaavy through a local webpage.

Playlists and individual links are supported for both buttons. Choose whether you want the full MP4 video or an MP3 audio-only export below.
